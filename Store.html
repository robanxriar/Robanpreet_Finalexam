About Us

Haldiram’s, with head office in Nagpur, was founded in 1937 by Shri Ganga Bhishen Agarwal, fondly known as Haldiram in his household; as a retail sweet and namkeen shop in Bikaner, Rajasthan. Haldiram’s in 1970 opened the first full-production unit in Nagpur to introduce a delectable variety of savories, sweets, and beverages to the market.
